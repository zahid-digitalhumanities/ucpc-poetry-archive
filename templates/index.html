📖 Research Contribution

UCPC is a research-grade Digital Humanities infrastructure for Urdu literary studies. The platform combines corpus linguistics, stylometry, semantic embeddings, machine learning, and computational text analysis to support large-scale exploration of classical and modern Urdu poetry.

The system is designed for researchers, students, and general readers interested in AI-assisted literary analysis, authorship attribution, intertextuality, and computational philology.
